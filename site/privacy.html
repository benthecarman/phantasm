Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 2, 2026

Short version: we don't collect anything. Phantasm has no accounts, no analytics, no telemetry, and no servers of ours in the middle. Your conversations exist in exactly two places: on your device, and on the backend server you configure and control.

What the app collects

Nothing. Phantasm's App Store privacy manifest declares zero collected data types and no tracking, and the app contains no analytics, crash reporting, advertising, or tracking SDKs. We — the developers — have no way to see your conversations, your settings, or even whether you use the app at all.

Where your data lives

  • On your device. Conversations are stored locally. Deleting a chat, or the app, deletes them.
  • On your server. When you send a message, it goes only to the backend URL you entered in Settings — typically a machine on your own network. What happens there is governed by you, because you run it.

Device permissions

Phantasm can optionally use device capabilities as tools for the AI model. Every one of them is off by default, enabled per-chat by you, and sends data only to your own configured backend:

  • Health — read-only, shared with the model only when you turn on the Health tool for a chat and the model requests it. The app never writes to Apple Health.
  • Location — shared only when you turn on the Location tool for a chat and the model requests it.
  • Calendar — events are shared only when you turn on the Calendar tool; creating an event always requires your confirmation.
  • Camera & photo library — used only when you attach a photo to a message or save a generated image.
  • Microphone & speech recognition — used for dictation; transcription runs on-device.

This website

getphantasm.com is a static site. It sets no cookies and runs no analytics or tracking scripts. It is served by Cloudflare, which processes standard request data (such as IP addresses) to deliver and secure the site, per Cloudflare's privacy policy.

Third parties

Apple may collect standard App Store install and diagnostics data under your device's analytics settings; that collection is governed by Apple's privacy policy, not ours. Beyond distribution through the App Store, Phantasm involves no third-party services — the backend it talks to is yours.

Changes

If this policy changes, the update will be published on this page. The app and this site are open source, so every change is also visible in the public repository.

Contact

Questions about privacy? Open an issue on GitHub.
